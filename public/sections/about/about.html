About LawLink

LawLink is a next-generation legal tech platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and accessible justice. Our mission is to simplify legal processes using AI, real-time tools, and innovative legal tech solutions.

Our Vision

We believe legal help should be fast, accessible, and secure for everyone. LawLink empowers individuals with tools to:

  • File harassment complaints easily
  • Use AI to understand legal rights and generate legal documents
  • Activate SOS and real-time location sharing for emergencies
  • Log audio evidence silently for protection

Key Features

AI Legal Assistant

Get instant legal answers with section references from IPC, CrPC, and other Indian laws.

Complaint Builder

Generate formal legal complaints with AI assistance and submit them with evidence.

SOS & Live Tracking

Trigger real-time location sharing in emergencies with a simple button hold.

Voice Log System

Record silent audio logs for legal use without attracting attention.
